Hoya Kerrii

Saterday, September 21st 2024

By Lisa Wu

  • Scientific name：

    Hoya Kerrii

  • Common name ：

    Sweetheart Plant, Valentine Hoya

Description

  • A climbing plant that can grow up to 4 meters high
  • Stems have a diameter of 7 mm. The leaves are 6 cm wide, 5 mm thick.
  • Adult plants show inflorescences of 5 cm diameter and up to 25 flowers. They produce small balls of nectar, coloured red to brown. Smell only faintly or not at all.
  • Also comes in a variegated version, known as the Variegated Sweetheart Plant. Each leaf displays unpredictable unique variegation patterns and colors on its leaves, adding delightful details for plant enthusiasts to enjoy.

Cultivation

  • Heat-tolerant but the ideal growing environment is on a bright balcony, with 2-4 hours of sunlight per day.
  • Growth slows down in winter, but a slightly higher humidity level will help boost growth.
  • Generally resistant to pests and diseases
  • With only moderate water needs.
  • Propagation is typically done by cuttings, using a stem with at least three leaves. Keep the medium slightly moist and the surrounding air humid for best results.

Application

  • Popular as a houseplant and is often given as a gift, symbolizing love and affection. Its unique shape adds a charming, decorative touch to indoor spaces.
  • Helps improve indoor air quality by absorbing pollutants and releasing oxygen. Its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities make it a great addition to homes and offices for a healthier indoor environment.
